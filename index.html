A little about me

Chicago has been my home for nearly 10 years, and so has my career in technology. I started out as an Apple Genius, then transitioned to IT help desk support. These experiences stepped up my passion to work on a team to solve any problem thrown my way. That's why this next chapter of my career feels so exciting and full of possibilities.


Growing up in the rural suburbs of Pittsburgh, PA, I had no idea how eager I'd be to explore my world on foot. One of my greatest loves is walking wherever my feet take me and snapping photos along the way. There's never a shortage of places to go, neighborhoods to see, breweries to visit, and pizza to eat. Chicago's lakefront especially charges me up.


This industry may be new to me, but it sparks a curiosity in me. I want to use that curiosity to discover what new adventures lie ahead. I know there's still so much to learn, but I know my talent for collaborating sets me up with a solid footing.
